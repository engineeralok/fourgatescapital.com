How it's works?

Besnik has invested in over a hundred homes in the greater bay area, deploying partner agency as well as investment agency from friends and family. Besnik Agency has pursued a diverse set of strategies depending on the opportunities presented in the market and also the point in the real estate cycle the investment decision was being made in. These strategies include buy-fix-sell, opportunistically lending directly to investors, holding rentals, and participating in real estate syndications.
